Pumperly is an open-source fuel and EV route planner with real-time prices across 36 countries. Find the cheapest fuel stations and EV charging points along your route.

Features:
- Real-time fuel prices across 36 European countries
- EV charging station locations via Open Charge Map
- Route planning with cheapest fuel stops
- Offline support with cached data
- Dark mode
- Deep link support
- Geolocation for nearby stations
- Material Design with splash screen

Pumperly uses OpenStreetMap data and can connect to your own self-hosted Pumperly instance or the public pumperly.com service.
